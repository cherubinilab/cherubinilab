Research Lines

The lab investigates the cortical and sub-cortical
brain regions at various scales ranging from individual synapses to large scale networks

Overview of Techniques

Over the course of 3 decades, the lab has adapted various state-of-the-art techniques to unravel the principles of neural computation

  • Patch clamp from acute brain slices
  • In-vivo recordings during anethetized and head-restrained conditions
  • In-vivo recordings from freely behaving animals
  • Optogenetic and chemogenetic interventions combined with eletrophysiology and behavior
  • Immunohistochemistry
